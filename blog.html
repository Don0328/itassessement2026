My 5-Step Glass Skin Morning Routine

April 2026  |  Beauty

Glass skin does not happen by accident! After months of experimenting, I have finally cracked my perfect 5-step morning skincare routine. It starts with a gentle foam cleanser, followed by a hydrating toner, a vitamin C serum, moisturiser, and of course, SPF. The key is layering thin products and giving each layer time to absorb before the next one.

Building a Minimal Pink Makeup Bag for University

March 2026  |  Beauty

Who has time for a full glam routine between back-to-back lectures? Not me. I narrowed my university makeup bag down to just six products: a tinted moisturiser, concealer, brow gel, a rosy blush, clear lip gloss, and setting spray. The result is a natural, put-together look that takes under ten minutes and stays fresh through the longest of class days.

How I Stay Organised as a University Student (Pink Edition)

February 2026  |  Academic Life

Staying on top of university deadlines, readings, and group projects while keeping your sanity intact is an art form. My system involves a pink bullet journal for weekly planning, a colour-coded Google Calendar, and a simple daily to-do list written every morning before I leave for campus. The aesthetic part? Every single planner, pen, and sticky note I own is in some shade of pink. It genuinely makes studying feel less painful.
