My Story

Hi gorgeous! I'm Bibhuti, a lover of most of the things pink, sparkly, and beautiful. I started Don & Her Idea because I wanted a space where girls could come to feel inspired, uplifted, and totally at home. Whether you're here for the beauty tips, the blog posts, or just the pretty vibes, I'm so glad you found me!

I'm based in Kathmandu, Nepal 🇳🇵, and I spend my days obsessing over skincare, journaling, and finding the prettiest cafes in town. This site is my little digital diary and you are most welcome to it! 💖

My Values

Kindness, authenticity, self-love and spreading positivity everywhere I go. I believe every girl deserves to feel beautiful and seen.

My Passions ✈️

Skincare, makeup, journaling, aesthetic photography, cosy reading nooks, and adventurous travel.

My Mission

To create a warm, judgement-free corner of the internet where you can be your most authentic, glittery self.
